O-LEVEL PROGRAMME

The school runs Nigeria’s Junior & Senior Secondary School national curricula, Also the British curriculum is used to prepare interested candidates for the IGCSE examinations.

A-LEVEL PROGRAMME

The school runs an intensive, result-oriented and character-building one-year acceleration Advanced Level Programme within its serene environment inside the University of Ibadan campus.

SAT

The school runs an intensive class on SAT - Scholastic Aptitude Test. Standardized test widely used for the United States and Canadian University admissions
